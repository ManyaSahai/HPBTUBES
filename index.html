Our Motto

To Engineer, Fabricate, Guarantee our Products, Deliver and Erect them at the right time is what we shall Excel at.

HPB is a trusted manufacturer and erector of IBR-approved boiler pressure parts, delivering custom-fabricated tubes, coils, headers, and heavy structures engineered to fit any steam generator. With 50+ years of combined expertise, we specialize in precision fabrication, bending, and welding of carbon, alloy, and stainless-steel components.

We provide innovative product solutions for all sustainable progress partners.

Premium Steel Solutions

Industry-leading tubes and pipes engineered to global standards with precision and reliability

⛓️

Material Grades & Types

Steel tubes and pipes in various chemistries:

  • Carbon Steel: Low, medium, and high carbon, with manganese
  • Alloy Steel: Chrome & Moly based grades
  • Stainless Steel: Nickel, chrome, moly compositions
  • Tubes: Heat transfer through walls (boilers, heaters, exchangers)
  • Pipes: Fluid and steam transfer through bore (pipelines, process systems)
⚡

Wide Dimensional Range

  • Outer Diameter: 8mm to 1016mm
  • Wall Thickness: 1mm to 76.2mm
  • Certifications: MTC, IBR Certified
⚙️

Manufacturing Methods

Precision-built products in both seamless and welded formats:

  • Seamless: Hot Finished (HFS), Cold Drawn (CDS)
  • Welded: ERW, SAW, CDW, SW, MMAW
🏗️

Key Applications

  • Steam and Line Piping
  • Boilers and Superheaters
  • Economizers and Water Walls
  • Headers and Air Pre-heaters
  • Heat Exchangers and Rifle Bore Tubes
📜

Global Standards Compliance

Our products comply with international specifications:

  • ASME / ASTM: A178, A179, A192, A210, A213, A312, A335, A106, A53, A333
  • DIN & EN: DIN 17175, EN 10216-2, St35.8, 16Mo3, 13CrMo4-5, X20CrMoV11-1
  • BS & Others: BS 3059, BS 6323, GB 5310 (20G, 15CrMoG), GOST Grades
